## Challenge: Create Your Hero

### 📋 Your Task

At the end, print a neatly formatted hero card showing everything.

| Rogue | 20 | 12 | "Strikes fast — chance to deal double damage" |
| Anything else | 20 | 10 | "A true wildcard" |

### 🏘️ The Hero's Background

| Background | Stat Bonus | Origin Story |
|---|---|---|
| Noble | +3 Attack | "Born into wealth, " + hero_name + " seeks something gold cannot buy." |
| Orphan | +5 Health | "With nothing to lose, " + hero_name + " fights for a future they never had." |
| Merchant | +2 Health, +2 Attack | "Sharp eyes and sharper deals prepared " + hero_name + " for the road ahead." |
| Anything else | No bonus | "A mysterious past follows " + hero_name + " wherever they go." |

| Combination | Title |

| Mage + Noble | "Arcane Lord" |

| Mage + Orphan | "The Forsaken Mage" |
| Warrior + Merchant | "Iron Merchant" |
| Rogue + Merchant | "The Cunning Trader" |
| Any other combination | "Adventurer" |
